Visit the national park Uruapan Michoacán

It’s one of the most important attractions in this city. In here you can admire the abundance of natural resources that exist throughout the region. People from other states, especially from the north, remain astonished at the huge amount and flow of water that embellishes this park.


It boasts an artificial fish pond.


Located very close to downtown, the National Park can be easily reached by foot. Enjoy the walk along the quiet streets of this Mexican province. There are some traditional food and bread stores on the way from La Pergola to the Park.


The flora groups 89 species of plants. Among them, you can see ferns, orchids and even ficus. The height of the park trees ranges from 4-30 meters.


This place is great for bird observations, you can catch turtle doves, owls, parrots, springs, swallows, hummingbirds, and others.


Some reptiles that wander around are burrowing snakes, salamanders, frogs, and even toads. Additionally you can see squirrels, opossums, armadillos, and sometimes bat mammals


The river's name comes from two P'urhepecha Cupatitzio words KUPATZINI and ITZIO, which mean "to souse" and "in water" respectively.


Don't miss it if you visit Uruapan City.
